1.Overview
 DSEG Font Family is free font to imitate LCD segment display. 

        <7SEG font>
         DSEG7-Classic
               Classic MINI
               Modern
               Modern MINI
               7SEGGCHAN
               7SEGGCHAN MINI

	<14SEG font>
         DSEG14-Classic
                Classic MINI
                Modern
                Modern MINI

	<LCD-like weather icons>
	 DSEG-Weather

2.Usage
 Please visit to DSEG support page.
 http://www.keshikan.net/fonts-e.html

3.Specifications
 * Classic and Modern styles include the roman-alphabet and number glyphs. 7SEGG-CHAN includes only "1234567890-:.".
 * 6 styles(Light, Light Italic, Regular, Italic, Bold, Bold Italic) are available on Classic and Modern types.
 * Some Fonts, named "-Mini", have bolder intervals between segments. You can use them on small space.
